لغات و اصطلاحات مرتبط با غذا


در این صفحه برخی از عبارات پر کاربرد انگلیسی درارتباط با غذا همراه با مثال گردآوری شده است.



  • a small/big eater = کم غذا/زیادخورI am a big eater unlike my wide who is a small eater.
  • dine in/out = غذا خونه یا بیرون خوردنI mostly dine in throughout the weekdays, but I enjoy dining out once a week too.
  • fussy eater = بد غذا My husband is a fussy eater, and he is hardly pleased with my cooking.
  • home-cooked food = غدای خانگی I greatly enjoy eating home-cooked food, especially my mom's.
  • local cuisine = غذای محلیYou must sample the local cuisine.
  • mouth-watering = خوشمزه
    Look at those mouth-watering cakes.
  • nutritious food = غذای مغذی It's is important to get plenty of exercise and to eat the nutritious foods.
  • quality justifies the bill = الکی گرون نیست I first thought that restaurant was too expensive, but when I tasted its food I realized that their quality justified the bill.
  • quick snack = غذای سبک و فوری Eating quick snack instead of main meal can be harmful for stomach.
  • ready meal = غذای آماده شده کنسروی I had no desire to cook, so I bought a ready meal in a nearby supermarket.
  • slap-up meal = غذای درست حسابی I feel like making a slap-up meal tonight. 
  • starving hungry =   خیلی گرسنه I woke up starving hungry yesterday and ate the whole roast chicken.
  • a take-away = غذای سفارش داده شده از بیرون I'm going to call a fastfood restaurant and order a takeaway.
  • the main meal = وعده اصلیMy main meal is lunch.
  • to be full-up = سیر بودن - Would you like some more?
    - No, thank you, I'm full-up!
  • to be starving hungry = ضعف کردن از گرسنگیI haven't eaten all day. I'm starving hungery!
  • to bolt something down = بلیعدن یا سریع خوردن غذا Don't bolt your food down like that, it's very rude!
  • have/grab a bite = یک لقمه بزنCome here and have a bite!
  • to foot the bill = صورتحساب غذا را پرداخت کردن You paid for dinner last time. Let me foot the bill this time.
  • spoil your appetite = اشتها را بردن Stop talking about the snails, you're spoiling my appetite!
  • to work up an appetite = اشتها را باز کردن He must have worked up an appetite in the gym.
